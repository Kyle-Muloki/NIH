Networking in Heels

We are a vibrant community where ambitious women unite to forge meaningful connections, share knowledge, and inspire each other's success.

Our Story

It all began with a deep-rooted belief in the power of connections. Having worked in various sectors for over a decade, I experienced firsthand the transformative influence of networking on personal and professional growth. The idea crystallized – I wanted to create a space where women could come together, not just for business but to build lasting friendships, foster career growth, and support each other’s dreams. Networking In Heels is my passion project, born out of a desire to empower women via meaningful connections to encourage career and business growth, foster entrepreneurship, especially among the youth.
I firmly believe in the importance of social capital and the profound impact it can have on our lives.

Our why

Mission

Empowering and connecting women to build meaningful relationship, achieve personal and professional growth and foster positive change in their lives and communiity .

Vision

To be a globally recognized platform founded on the principles of Networking in Heels, led by Violette Wambua, that inspire and support women to excel in their careers, businesses and personal lives through the power of networking, mentorship and collaboration
